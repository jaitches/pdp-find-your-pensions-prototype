Introduction to consent

Consent is a necessary part of using digital services where data sharing is required (you may have seen it with cookies). In order to keep your information secure and keep you in control of its usage we have a number of consents you can agree or disagree to. Please note that if you disagree to some consents you will not be able to access some functionalities of the service.

Consent to an authorised person having access to view your pensions

If you agree to this, the authorised person will be able to view your pensions via a software provider for the period of time you have said. They will only be able to see the pensions that you selected.,

If you do not agree to this, the Money and Pensions Service will not allow the authorised person you selected access to view your pensions.

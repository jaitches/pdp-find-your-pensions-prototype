Giving access to view your pensions

You can allow an authorised person to view your pensions if they have asked you to do so.

An authorised person can be:

  • a financial advisor
  • someone who gives guidance on behalf of the Money and Pensions Service

The person you delegate access to will only be able to view the same pension information as you. They will not be able to carry out any transactions on your pensions.

If someone already has access to view your pensions you can remove their access by withdrawing your consent

Verify your identity

To set up an identity account, you need to provide evidence for the company to check.

When creating the account you'll be asked for:

  • a valid passport
  • a valid driving licence, full or provisional, with your photo on it

The companies can check your passport and driving licence against official records. They can also confirm your personal details by accessing information like credit records. This will help them be sure it's really you.

If you only have one photo identity document (ID), you'll also need a phone or a tablet to download a free app and take pictures of it. This will let you prove the ID is yours.
